reference : english grammar in use

that sinon.
that est plus usuel que which .
on ne peut pas utiliser that dans certains cas :
exemple :
John, who (pas that) speaks French and Italian, works as a tourist guid.
Colin told me about his new job,which (pas that) he's enjoying very much.
